How to play

Rock, Paper, Scissors is a game of CHANCE in which you choose an object to combat the computers object. The winner is the first score to reach 5.

Rock - Choosing Rock will win the round if the computer picks Scissors.

Paper - Choosing Paper will win the round if the computer picks Rock.

Scissors - Choosing Scissors will win the round if the computer picks Paper.
